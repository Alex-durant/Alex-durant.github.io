Hi I'm Alex! This is my last semester at Asnuntuck before I transfer to UCONN. I only have this class and three others to go! I am currently working towards an associates in Communications: Broadcasting, but my goal is to get a job in animal care. I have lived with both dogs and cats, but I also want to learn more about reptiles, anphibians, and larger mammals. I realized this was what I wanted to devote my life to two years ago after helping a friend rehabilitate a sick and previously feral cat. Experiencing her growth firsthand was an extremely rewarding experience. I enjoy learning and am always looking forward to furthering my knowledge on any given topic. One of my main hobbies is discovering everything I can about fictional stories conveyed through liturature, video games, and television. I try to participate in as many creative events that celebrate such media as I can.
